Oral rehabilitation

It is a comprehensive approach that seeks to restore the function and aesthetics of the mouth. It includes treatments such as the placement of crowns, bridges and prostheses to improve oral health and restore the smile of patients.

Implantology

It focuses on the placement of dental implants, which are titanium structures designed to replace missing teeth. This service offers permanent and stable solutions to restore the functionality and aesthetics of the mouth.

Fixed orthodontics

It is a treatment that corrects the alignment of the teeth and the bite by placing brackets and wires. This service is essential to achieve a straight smile and a proper bite.

Endodontics

It focuses on the treatment of diseases and injuries of the dental pulp (the internal tissue of the tooth). This service includes root canal treatments to save teeth that would otherwise be lost.

Dental surgery

He addresses a variety of surgical procedures in the mouth, such as wisdom tooth extractions, dental implant surgery, and correction of periodontal problems. It provides effective solutions for various dental problems.

Restorative and aesthetic

It focuses on improving the appearance of the teeth and smile. Includes services such as teeth whitening, veneers and dental restorations to achieve a more beautiful and healthy smile.
